Loft

Design a 3D kayak hull from a curved spine (rocker) and a set of cross-sections, lofted live. Drag the 3D view to rotate · scroll to zoom · double-click to reset.

Three governing curves: rocker (blue, on-curve knots — click to add), deck line (green, off-curve handles), beam line (teal, top view). Hull converges to a point at stern and bow. Stations define cross-section shapes — add/remove via the panel. Click rocker to insert a shape knot.
