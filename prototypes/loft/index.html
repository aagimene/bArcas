Loft

Design a 3D kayak hull from a curved spine (rocker) and a set of cross-sections, lofted live. Drag the 3D view to rotate · scroll to zoom · double-click to reset.

Phase C — section editor. Side view: drag spine points to reshape the rocker, drag station ticks to reposition stations. Cross-section view: drag points to reshape the selected section, click empty space to add a point, right-click a point to delete (keel point is locked at the spine). The 3D loft rebuilds live. Add/remove stations and chine flags arrive in phases D – E.
