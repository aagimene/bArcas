Loft

Design a 3D kayak hull from a curved spine (rocker) and a set of cross-sections, lofted live. Drag the 3D view to rotate · scroll to zoom · double-click to reset.

Phase F — four-line loft governance. The hull is defined by four curves: rocker/keel (blue Bézier), deck line (green), bow sheer keel (orange), stern sheer keel (purple). Drag the ring-style tip points to position the bow/stern convergence; click near a sheer keel line to add interior control points; right-click to delete. The teal tick sets where the rocker hands off to the sheer keel. Interior stations have keel locked to rocker and deck locked to deck line. Sheer-end stations (via + add station) have keel locked to sheer keel line, deck locked to deck line.
